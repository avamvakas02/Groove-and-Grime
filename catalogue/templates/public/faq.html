Store Story

What can I do as a Visitor?

You can browse the full collection, filter records, and view detailed information. Visitor accounts cannot purchase from the crate.

What is included in Pro and Pro+?

Pro unlocks crate access and purchasing. Pro+ includes everything in Pro plus premium vault-oriented benefits listed on the pricing page.

How do I upgrade my membership?

Go to the Pricing page and choose your tier. If you are signed in, the appropriate upgrade button will guide you through the change.

Can I downgrade from Pro or Pro+?

Downgrades are currently disabled in-app. Use Contact support if you need a plan change review.

Why is the crate icon locked or inactive?

The crate is fully available for Pro and Pro+ members. Visitor accounts can browse but must upgrade to buy.

How do I add records to my crate?

Open any eligible record and use the add-to-crate action. Your crate badge in the navbar updates with total items.

Can I update quantities before checkout?

Yes. On the crate page, use quantity controls to increase, reduce, or remove records before confirming the order.

What if a record becomes unavailable?

Stock can change quickly for rare releases. If an item is unavailable during checkout, remove it and continue with available records.

How do I add records to wishlist?

In Collection, click the heart icon on any record card. A filled heart means that record is already in your wishlist.

How do I remove records from wishlist?

Click the filled heart again in Collection, or remove items directly from your Wishlist page.

Where can I see my reviews?

Open your profile dropdown and go to Reviews. You can track your posted opinions from there.

I cannot find a record I saw before. What should I do?

Try clearing filters, adjusting sort, and searching by artist/title spelling variations. Rare releases may rotate in availability.

How do I edit my account details?

Open your profile menu and select My Account to update your profile information.

How do I keep my account secure?

Use a strong unique password, avoid shared devices without logging out, and always sign out after public computer sessions.

Can I use one account on multiple devices?

Yes, you can sign in from multiple devices, but for safety we recommend logging out from devices you do not control.

I forgot where key pages are located. Quick recap?

Collection is for discovery, Pricing is for tier management, Wishlist stores favorites, Reviews shows your feedback history.

Who do I contact for billing questions?

Use the Contact page and include your account username plus a short description of the issue for faster support.

How long does support usually take?

Response times vary by request volume, but account and payment issues are prioritized.

Can I report bugs or broken pages?

Absolutely. Share what happened, the page where it occurred, and steps to reproduce it through Contact.

Do you have more policy details?

Policy-specific updates are communicated through site notices and support responses when relevant.
